## Key Components of a Humanoid Robot

| Part    | Function | Common Technologies |
|--------|--------|---------------------|
| **Head** | Perception & interaction | Cameras (eyes), microphones (ears), speakers (voice), LED displays (expressions) |
| **Arms** | Manipulation & gestures | Joints (shoulder, elbow, wrist), grippers or hands, force/torque sensors |
| **Legs** | Locomotion & mobility | Hip/knee/ankle joints, motors, balance control systems |
| **Torso** | Core support & integration | Houses battery, computer, and communication systems; critical for balance |

Together, these parts enable complex behaviors like:
- Walking on uneven terrain  
- Picking up a cup without crushing it  
- Turning the head to look at someone speaking  
- Waving hello or shaking hands
